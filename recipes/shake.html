Strawberry Smoothie

You'll love this icy cold strawberry smoothie

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • half a cup of plain yogurt
  • half a cup of skim milk
  • 3 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes of ice, crushed
  1. Step 1: In a blender combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla. Toss in the ice. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and serve.
